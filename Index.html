FAQ

How far in advance should I book?

For most cakes we recommend at least 7 days. For weddings or complex designs, 4–8 weeks is ideal.

Do you take a deposit?

Yes — a 50% non-refundable deposit secures your date. The balance is due on collection.

Can you match colours or an inspo photo?

Absolutely. Share your palette or photos and we’ll adapt them to a cohesive, food-safe design.

Do you offer tastings?

For wedding cakes we can arrange a tasting box subject to availability — ask when you enquire.
