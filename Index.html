About Us

FAQ & Policies

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2–3 weeks in advance, though wedding cakes may require 2–3 months. Rush orders are sometimes possible (fee applies).

Do you deliver?

Currently we only offer collection from Salford, Manchester. For large wedding cakes we may be able to arrange delivery within Greater Manchester — please ask.

Do you cater to allergies?

We can adjust most recipes to be nut-free, gluten-free or dairy-free. However, all cakes are baked in the same kitchen where allergens are present, so trace risk remains.

What’s the difference between sponge layers and tiers?

Sponge layers are the individual cake sections stacked with filling inside a single cake. Tiers are separate cakes stacked on top of each other to create a taller, multilevel cake.

What’s your cancellation policy?

Deposits are non-refundable. If you cancel with more than 7 days’ notice, we may transfer your deposit to a future booking. Cancellations under 7 days may forfeit payments.
